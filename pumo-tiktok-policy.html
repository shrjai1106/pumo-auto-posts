Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

This application accesses only the TikTok account credentials necessary to publish video content to our own official PUMO Technovation TikTok account. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from TikTok users or viewers.

2. How We Use Information

Account access tokens obtained through TikTok OAuth are used solely to publish educational video content to our own TikTok account. These tokens are stored securely as encrypted environment variables and are never shared with third parties.

We do not store any user data, viewer data, or TikTok platform data beyond the access tokens required for our own account authentication. Access tokens are stored as encrypted secrets in our private GitHub repository.

5. Data Retention

Access tokens are retained only as long as necessary for the application to function. They can be revoked at any time through the TikTok developer portal.
